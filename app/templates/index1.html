A Virtual Trial Room utilizes advanced technologies such as, artificial intelligence (AI), and 3D modeling to create an immersive shopping environment. This technology allows customers to visualize how clothing will look and fit on their bodies without needing to be physically present in a store.

By leveraging their smartphones or computers, users can upload their images or create 3D avatars that accurately represent their body shapes and sizes. This personalized approach enhances the online shopping experience by providing a realistic simulation of how different garments will fit.
